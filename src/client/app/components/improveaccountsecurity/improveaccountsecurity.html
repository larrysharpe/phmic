Improve Account Security

Security is important. If you want to increase the security of your account, consider activating some of thw following features.

SMS Authentication Currently Active

With SMS authentication activated, you'll be required to enter a one-time code any time you want to log into your account. We'll text the code to you when you try to access your account.

Recovery Email Verified

It's a good idea to have a backup email address attached to your account, in case you ever need to recover your account. This can't match the email you use to log in.
